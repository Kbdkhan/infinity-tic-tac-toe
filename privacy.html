PRIVACY PROTOCOLS

LAST UPDATED: JANUARY 2026 //

Welcome to Infinity Tic-Tac-Toe ("we," "our," or "us"). Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you visit our website infinitytictactoe.com.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in minimal data collection. We do not require you to create an account, register, or provide any personal identification (PII) to play our game.

2. Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted third-party services to ensure the smooth operation of our website:

  • GitHub Pages: Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect server logs (IP addresses) for security and reliability purposes. You can read GitHub's Privacy Statement here.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how players interact with our game (e.g., which difficulty mode is most popular, session duration). GA4 uses cookies to collect anonymous usage data. It does not store your IP address.

3. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use them strictly for:

  • Analytics: To help us improve the game experience.
  • Performance: To remember your preferences (like volume settings) locally on your device.

You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings, though this may reset your game preferences.

4. Children's Privacy

Our game is safe for all ages, but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.
